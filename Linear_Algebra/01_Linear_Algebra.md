## Vectors
### What even is vector?

> **An Arrow in Coordinate Space** 
> **note:- In Linear Algebra, Vector will always be rooted at the origin.**

#### **How To Write A Vector**

$$\boxed{\begin{matrix} X-coords\\Y-coords\\Z-coords \end{matrix}}$$
This is how we write vector in *Mathematics.*

---
### Vector Addition 

#### How Do We Add Vectors?

>Put tail of second vector on head of first vector.

$$\boxed{\vec{r} = \vec{r_x} + \vec{r_y}}$$

This is how we add vector components.
#### **Vector Example**

![Vector Example][https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcQOyCCdiyb7_O7gQ9kf8MoHThGytskwnpKM1JWgD_0ZaSFVze7-1KKnz_Q&s=10 | 200]

#### Why Do We Even Add Like This?

Think of it like this, Vectors Represent **Movement.** 
And when we observe the movement of those two vectors (E.g.- $\vec{r_x}  ,  \vec{r_y}$).
The resultant comes in direction of vector $\vec{r}$.
